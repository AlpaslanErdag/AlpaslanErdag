### 🔬 Academic Research & Papers
I am currently a **PhD Candidate in Artificial Intelligence**, focusing on bridging the gap between unstructured reasoning and structured knowledge. My ongoing academic research and upcoming papers cover:

* **Graph-Based RAG Architectures:** Investigating the optimization of Retrieval-Augmented Generation through Knowledge Graphs to improve context awareness in LLMs.
* **Autonomous Agent Orchestration:** Researching multi-agent frameworks for complex task decomposition and self-correcting reasoning loops.
* **Holistic System Architectures:** Developing integrated frameworks for autonomous LLM agents to manage long-term memory and networked knowledge.
* **Secure AI Deployment:** Exploring the challenges and methodologies of deploying large-scale models in closed-system environments.
